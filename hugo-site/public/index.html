Hi, I’m David. This is the blog where I chart progress on the books that I read, and what I think about them. I self-host this blog. Self-hosting websites have the disadvantage that sometimes they are not available - uptime is not 100%, and search engines don’t like that aspect. However I feel it is worth it for the learning experience and the control I have over every aspect of my sites. If you need to contact me at any time, please use: 3rp2mzocr@mozmail.com.
